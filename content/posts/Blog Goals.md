# Hello World!

Someone said somewhere that you should always start with `Hello World!` so here it is.

## Blog goals

This is not the first time I've tried to start a blog and likely this won't be the last, but I'm hoping that this will be a more serious attempt at keeping going.

I feel like that I've been lacking in the writing department, most people that inspire me spend considerable time writing things down, things they learn, things they are thinking about and things they are still figuring out. I feel like I don't spend enough time writing things down and while I'm able to remember quite a lot of things, the amount of stuff that I end up forgetting is at least 100x greater.

I don't want this to become a dump of every little thing that goes trough my mind, but I would like it to be a place where I can post things that might help me remember stuff or maybe help others understand something.

Someone said that writing down your thoughts can help you make sense of them, the simple act of having put them to written words forces you to put your ideas into a shape that is somewhat cohesive. (I can certainly feel it already even just writing down what I would like this website to be)

## Technology used

* [Obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md) - Writing down each post.
* [Obsidian Hugo Plublish](https://github.com/kirito41dd/obsidian-hugo-publish) - Obsidian 3rd party plugin used to synchronise relevant Obsidian MD files into Hugo folder structure.
* [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) - Static website generator.
* [Blowfish](https://blowfish.page) - Pre-made customisable theme for Hugo.
* [GitHub](https://github.com) - Hosting the code repo for the website and its content.
* [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com) - Automated website build and website hosting.
